Thanks for visiting my corner. I am a PhD student in theoretical physics at the École Normale Supérieure–PSL, Paris, working under the supervision of Professor Adam Nahum. I graduated with a Master's from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai, in the Department of Theoretical Physics.

My primary interests lie in statistical and condensed matter physics, pariticularly phase transitions in various settings. Currently, I am working on measurement induced transition in classical lattice systems and trying to understand the charactersitics of the phase transitions better. At TIFR, I worked with Professor Kedar Damle on disordered systems and Ising models on hyperbolic lattices.

I actively engage in the online mathematics discussion forum MathOverflow.

I love to watch movies (you can connect me Letterboxd on Letterboxd), read books (Goodreads), click pictures, watch football, sometimes draw, and love to play GeoGuessr.
